Investment and Financial Decision-Making 投资与财务决策

Learn to make investment decisions by connecting goals, evidence, risk and portfolio choices. 学习把目标、证据、风险与投资组合选择联系起来，形成有依据的投资决策。

We use fictional family cases and team SMG portfolios—never real family account details or personal investment advice.课程使用虚构家庭案例和团队SMG投资组合，不收集真实家庭账户信息，也不提供个人投资建议。

Course route 课程路线

Where the course goes 课程学习路线

Six units move from goals to markets, analysis and portfolio decisions.六个单元从投资目标逐步进入市场、分析与投资组合决策。

  1. 01

    Personal Investment Foundations

    个人投资基础

    Goals, horizon, compounding, inflation and risk.目标、期限、复利、通胀与风险。

  2. 02

    Investment Choices for Families

    家庭投资选择

    Cash, bonds, shares, funds, fees and diversification.现金、债券、股票、基金、费用与分散投资。

  3. 03

    How Markets Work

    市场如何运作

    Issuance, trading, prices, returns, indices and news.发行、交易、价格、回报、指数与新闻。

  4. 04

    Analysing Companies

    公司分析

    Business models, statements, risk and valuation.商业模式、财务报表、风险与估值。

  5. 05

    Portfolios and Investor Behaviour

    投资组合与投资者行为

    Allocation, concentration, rebalancing and bias.资产配置、集中度、再平衡与行为偏差。

  6. 06

    Family Decisions and Careers

    家庭投资决策与职业

    Education, housing, retirement, careers and final strategy.教育、住房、退休、职业与最终策略。
