Investment Analysis Add-on Course

投资分析拓展课程

A 30-lesson extension course for listed-company evidence, retrieval practice, Section A-style worksheet questions and careful written analysis.

Syllabus overview

The standard Investment Analysis add-on course is built around company analysis. It gives learners a structured way to read company evidence, retrieve key knowledge, calculate simple measures, answer Section A-style evidence questions and write their own evidence-based classroom judgements with a practical next investment action.

The course:

  • introduces learners to investment terminology, company evidence and market data
  • develops learners' skills in using simple financial calculations to analyse and interpret information, as well as to organise and present ideas
  • explores links between risk, return, inflation, interest rates, costs, diversification and valuation
  • examines how regulation, exchange rates, debt, economic cycles and sector conditions affect company analysis
  • engages learners in applying evidence to company cases while distinguishing classroom judgement from personalised financial advice.

课程概览

投资分析拓展课程为希望理解股票、公司证据和市场价格讨论方式的学习者提供实践性基础。本课程不是 IGCSE 考纲，也不提供个人投资建议；它通过结构化方法拓展课堂学习，使学生能够阅读资料、进行简单计算，并围绕真实公司和基金写出均衡判断。

本课程：

  • 介绍投资术语、公司证据和市场数据
  • 培养学生运用简单金融计算来分析和解释信息，以及组织和表达观点的能力
  • 探究股价、预期、盈利能力、现金流、估值和风险之间的联系
  • 考察竞争、监管、汇率、债务和消费者需求变化如何影响公司与投资者
  • 引导学生把证据应用于当前公司案例，同时区分课堂分析与投资建议。
