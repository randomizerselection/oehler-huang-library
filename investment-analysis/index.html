Syllabus overview

Investment Analysis: Company Analysis

投资分析：公司分析

A 30-lesson company-analysis course where students move from first opinion to evidence-based judgement through short company dilemmas.

Decision-first syllabus overview

The standard route teaches students to improve a first classroom judgement with one key idea, one evidence task and one justified next action. Each lesson starts from a real company question, uses dated evidence, then asks students what a careful investor should do next.

The course:

  • Know what you are buying. Name the company, listing, stock code, fund or product and explain the ownership or exposure.
  • Check whether it fits the investor. Separate goals, time horizon, liquidity needs and risk level before judging possible return.
  • Read business evidence. Use dated figures and state what each source can and cannot prove.
  • Compare return, risk and price. Ask whether the price already reflects good news and whether the case is attractive, risky, too expensive or incomplete.
  • Choose the next action. Write a defensible classroom action: consider, watch, avoid, compare with another choice or gather more evidence.

课程概览

标准课程用 30 节课训练学生从第一印象走向有证据的判断。每节课从一个真实公司问题开始，学习一个关键概念，完成一个证据任务，最后写出一个有理由的下一步行动。

本课程：

  • 知道自己买的是什么。 说清公司、上市地点、股票代码、基金或产品，以及它代表的所有权或风险暴露。
  • 判断是否适合投资者。 先区分目标、时间长度、流动性需要和风险承受能力，再讨论可能回报。
  • 阅读公司证据。 使用有日期的数字，并说明证据能证明什么、不能证明什么。
  • 比较回报、风险和价格。 判断好消息是否已经反映在价格中，并说明案例是有吸引力、有风险、太贵还是证据不足。
  • 选择下一步行动。 写出可辩护的课堂行动：考虑、观察、避免、与另一个选择比较，或继续收集证据。

本课程只训练课堂中的证据分析，不提供股票推荐、市场择时或个人投资建议。
