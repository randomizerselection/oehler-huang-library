Your progress 学习成果

What you will learn 你将学会什么

Four capabilities connect all six units.四项核心能力贯穿六个单元。

  • Define the goal. Set the horizon, liquidity need and risk limits.明确目标：说明期限、流动性需求与风险限制。
  • Compare choices. Judge cash, bonds, shares and funds using the same criteria.比较选择：用统一标准评估现金、债券、股票与基金。
  • Use evidence. Explain markets and analyse companies using dated sources.运用证据：使用有日期的来源解释市场并分析公司。
  • Defend a portfolio decision. Connect goals, evidence, risk and review rules.论证投资组合决策：把目标、证据、风险与复查规则联系起来。

More SMG information 更多SMG信息Workbook route, evidence and student materials 学习手册路线、证据与学生资料
Permanent student record

The complete workbook stays in the course

Students complete or revisit the official workbook when its content fits the lesson. The page calendar follows the course concept sequence, so workbook pages are not always completed in numerical order.

Individual record

Workbook plus activity inserts

The official workbook is each student's default work record. Separately labelled activity inserts contain only evidence, calculations or judgements that its pages cannot hold.

Lesson handout

Bilingual exam-revision notes

Handouts use targeted English definition blanks completed during the lesson, complete Chinese meaning support and a short bilingual numbered list of examinable knowledge points. They contain no extended tasks or response spaces.

Team record

The decision log is authoritative

Only the shared team log authorises and records decisions, dissent, order checks, platform status and review triggers. A workbook answer never authorises a trade.

Course override

Trade prompts become decisions

Any instruction to buy or enter a trade means propose buy, hold, research, sell, rebalance or no trade. Orders still require the course approval gate.

Course alignment

Six phases across all fifty lessons

The official SMG sequence and the course concept sequence operate as a dual spine: investment ideas supply the method, and the live team portfolio supplies the recurring decision environment.

Assessment evidence

One evidence trail, six assessed outputs

Each lesson produces an individual workbook entry or activity insert. The handout remains a definition-and-numbered-point revision sheet. The team log adds a row when a decision or monitoring update occurs. Students then curate—not reproduce—that evidence for the unit assessment.

How lessons work 课程如何进行Try, learn, apply and decide 尝试、学习、应用与判断
Teaching model

Decision-first investment learning

Every lesson begins with a decision students can attempt, reveals the evidence that is missing and ends with a more defensible action or planning output.

Lesson rhythm

Hook, key idea, try it, decide

Retrieval, evidence checks, calculations and formative assessment sit inside one coherent investigation rather than appearing as disconnected activities.

Investment workflow

A repeatable decision process

The same workflow connects the family-goal lessons to market evidence, company analysis, portfolio decisions and the final capstone.

Course boundaries 课程边界Scope and advice limits 课程范围与建议限制
Course standards

Boundaries that keep the course useful

Personal relevance comes from family investment decisions, not from turning the course into general household administration.

In scope

Personal wealth decisions

Goals, horizons, risk, investment choices, markets, companies, portfolios, family cases and career foundations.

Out of scope

General consumer finance

No budgeting, payslips, consumer credit, insurance administration, tax administration or general banking units.

No-advice boundary

Teach method, not tips

Students may judge mock cases but do not recommend named products or tell real people what to buy, sell or hold.

Language support

English with concise Chinese support

Important questions, terms, definitions, tasks and model conclusions receive Simplified Chinese support.
