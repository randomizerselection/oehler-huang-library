SMG Workbook Course Guide SMG学习手册课程指南

Keep the complete SMG Essentials Workbook for the full course. Complete or revisit pages when your teacher assigns them; the course does not follow the workbook from front to back.完整保留SMG学习手册，并按照教师安排完成或重做指定页面；课程不按手册页码从头到尾推进。

Your individual record 个人学习记录

The workbook is your default work record. File any separately labelled activity inserts with the matching lesson pages. Lesson handouts use bilingual fill-in definitions and numbered revision points.学习手册是你的默认学习记录；活动补充单应与对应课程页面放在一起。课程讲义使用双语填空定义和编号复习要点。

Your team's official record 团队正式记录

The shared team decision log records evidence, decisions, order checks, platform status and review triggers.共享团队决策日志记录证据、决策、订单核查、平台状态和复查条件。

Trade instructions 交易指令

A workbook instruction never authorises a trade. It becomes a proposal and must pass the course approval gate.手册中的交易指令不能直接授权交易；它只能成为建议，并必须通过课程审批关卡。

Course rules 课程规则

    Workbook page calendar 学习手册页码安排

    Lessons not listed use one separately labelled activity insert filed with the workbook. No general worksheet should duplicate an official workbook activity, and the lesson handout contains knowledge rather than tasks.未列出的课程使用一页活动补充单，并与学习手册一起保存；不得另发重复官方手册活动的通用练习单，课程讲义只包含知识内容。
