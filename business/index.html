Unit 5 exam targets

Cambridge 0264 rewards applied business judgement. Unit 5 lessons combine Paper 1 short-answer chains with Paper 2 case decisions.

Paper 1

Short answer and data response

Four compulsory mini-context questions, 80 marks. Practice uses define, outline, explain and 8-mark justify patterns.

K App An Eval
Paper 2

Case study decision paper

One insert and four 20-mark questions. Practice uses applied 8-mark explanations and 12-mark recommendations.
